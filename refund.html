Refund & Returns Policy

Easy returns across Kuwait and the GCC. Here's exactly how it works.

Home / Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

1. Our promise

If something isn't right, we'll make it right. You may return most items within 14 days of delivery for a refund or exchange, provided they are unused, unworn and in their original packaging with tags attached.

2. How to start a return

Email hello@hafeez.shop with your order number, or bring your item to our booth inside Harley-Davidson Kuwait. We'll guide you through the next steps.

3. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, approved refunds are issued to your original payment method (KNET, card or Apple Pay) within 5–10 business days. Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

4. Exchanges

Need a different size or colour? We're happy to exchange eligible items subject to availability — the fastest option is usually to return for a refund and reorder.

5. Non-returnable items

  • Final-sale and clearance items marked non-returnable.
  • Personal/hygiene items where the seal has been broken (e.g. certain swimwear).
  • Gift cards.

6. Faulty or incorrect items

If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours and we'll arrange a free replacement or full refund, including shipping.

7. Contact

Need help? Reach us at hello@hafeez.shop or via our About page.
