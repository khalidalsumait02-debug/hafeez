# Hafeez — Website Design Plan (locked)

> Outcome of the design kickoff. Decisions below are agreed. Branding and catalog
> facts live in BRAND.md. Backend stays Shopify; this is a new custom storefront.

## Locked decisions

| Decision      | Choice                                                            |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Aesthetic     | **Balanced hybrid** — Hafeez green/yellow as the frame/buttons; products bring their own look (H-D leather/black, summer brights) |
| Navigation    | **Shop by world/brand** — Harley-Davidson · PaddleSmash · Summer & Lifestyle · Sale |
| Language      | **Bilingual EN + Arabic, RTL** at launch                          |
| Homepage hero | **Single bold statement** with two clear CTAs                     |
| Platform      | Keep **Shopify** backend; build a new **custom Shopify theme**    |

## Core idea
Lead with the most ownable asset: **Kuwait's official Harley-Davidson store, with
a booth inside H-D Kuwait** (authenticity + trust). Brand colors frame the site;
products perform inside it. PaddleSmash is the viral hook and gets its own world.

## Information architecture (top nav)
- **Harley-Davidson** (mega-menu → Men's, Women's, Headwear, Leather & Jackets,
  Accessories & Collectables, Gifts)
- **PaddleSmash**
- **Summer & Lifestyle** (SunnyLife, Vacay Swimwear, La Paz, DOIY)
- **Sale / Clearance**
- **About / Our Booth**
- Prominent search · cart · account · EN/AR toggle
- Clean up the 28 messy collections (kill app-junk: "Best Sellers" 1,139,

## New landing pages (current gap)

- **Harley-Davidson** — official-reseller story + shop by category
- **Summer & Lifestyle** — seasonal hub

## Collection & product page upgrades
- Collections: filters (size, price, brand, category), uniform grid, quick-add
- Products: large optimized images, size guides, clear KWD price, GCC delivery
  estimate, official-merch trust badge, related items, sticky mobile add-to-cart

## Design system
- Colors: green `#26d07c` (brand/header) · orange `#ff6900` (buttons/sale) ·
  dark green `#005844` (text/footer) · yellow `#fff19f` & mint `#b5e3d8`
  (accents/soft bg) · black (premium sections)
- Type: bold geometric sans headings (echoes the blocky logo) + clean body;
  Arabic font (e.g. Tajawal/Cairo) for RTL
- Mobile-first, generous whitespace, big consistent imagery, fast loading
- Low-maintenance: automated collections + swappable seasonal bands

## Next step (not started yet)
Per owner: do NOT start building. When given the go-ahead, first deliverable is a
homepage design concept for sign-off, then build out the custom Shopify theme.

## Audience by world (owner-confirmed) → tone
- **Harley-Davidson** = riders (older skew) → premium, rugged, heritage,
  aspirational rider lifestyle.
- **Vacay / La Paz** = summer & beach buyers → bright, sunny, vacation energy.
- **PaddleSmash + Lift Down** = 20–30 year olds → energetic, social,
  trend-forward, video-first, fun.
Each world keeps the shared green/yellow frame but tunes imagery/tone to its buyer.
