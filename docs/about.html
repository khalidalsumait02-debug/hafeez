Built for the way Kuwait lives

Hafeez International began inside Harley-Davidson Kuwait, bringing 100% authentic, officially licensed H-D® merchandise to riders across the GCC. Today we're also the regional home of the viral PaddleSmash game and a hand-picked roster of summer and lifestyle brands — Lift Down, Vacay, La Paz, SunnyLife and DOIY — curated for chalet weekends, pool days and the long Gulf summer.
