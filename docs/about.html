Kuwait's official Harley-Davidson® merchandise reseller — and home to the brands that own the summer.

Hafeez International is a subsidiary of Harley-Davidson Kuwait and the country's official reseller of authentic Harley-Davidson® merchandise. Alongside the legend of the open road, we curate the games, gear and labels that define a Kuwait summer.

Our story

What started inside the Harley-Davidson Kuwait showroom grew into something bigger: a single destination for genuine, officially licensed product across motorcycling heritage, racquet sports and coastal lifestyle. From the viral PaddleSmash game taking over chalets and beaches, to Lift Down headwear and the Summer & Lifestyle edit, every brand we carry earns its place.

What we stand for

  • 100% authentic. Every item is officially licensed — never replicas, never grey-market.
  • Built for the Gulf. Product chosen for the heat, the water and the way Kuwait actually lives.
  • Fast across the GCC. Tracked delivery to Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain and Oman.
  • Pay your way. KNET, Visa, Mastercard and Apple Pay, in Kuwaiti Dinar.
