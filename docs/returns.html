Returns & Refunds

Easy returns.
No fuss.

Changed your mind? You have 14 days to return unworn items in their original condition.

We want you to love what you ordered. If something isn't right, you have 14 days from delivery to start a return.

What can be returned

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed and in their original condition with all tags and packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (your order number or receipt) is required.
  • For hygiene reasons, swimwear must have the hygiene liner intact, and earrings cannot be returned.
  • Sale items marked final sale are not eligible unless faulty.

How to start a return

  1. Email support@hafeez.shop with your order number and the items you'd like to return.
  2. We'll reply with return instructions within one business day.
  3. Send the items back, or drop them at our showroom booth in Kuwait.

Refund policy

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll email you to confirm. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method — KNET, card or Apple Pay — within 5–7 business days. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the item was faulty or incorrect.

Exchanges

The quickest way to swap a size or colour is to return the original item for a refund and place a new order. If stock is limited, contact us first and we'll do our best to hold your size.

Faulty or incorrect items

If your item arrives damaged, faulty or not as ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo. We'll arrange a free return and a replacement or full refund, including any shipping you paid.
