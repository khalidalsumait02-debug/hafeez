Headwear

Most Lift Down and La Paz caps are one-size with an adjustable strap (fits approx. 55–61 cm). Fitted styles list their measurement on the product page.

How to measure

  • Chest / bust: measure around the fullest part, keeping the tape level.
  • Waist: measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist.
  • Head: measure around your forehead, just above the ears.
