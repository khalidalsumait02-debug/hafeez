Shipping & Delivery

Tracked delivery,
across the GCC.

Fast, tracked shipping from Kuwait to the wider Gulf — with free express delivery on orders over 25 KWD.

We ship from Kuwait to all six GCC countries. Every order is tracked end to end, and delivery over 25 KWD ships free.

Rates & timing

DestinationEstimated timeCost
Kuwait1–2 business daysFree over 25 KWD · otherwise 1.500 KWD
Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman3–5 business daysFree over 25 KWD · otherwise from 4.000 KWD

Orders are processed within one business day. Delivery estimates start once your order ships and exclude weekends and public holidays. Cross-border orders may be subject to local customs duties or taxes, payable by the recipient.

Track your order

As soon as your order ships, we email a tracking link to the address on your order. Use it to follow your parcel from our warehouse to your door.

Can't find the email? Check your spam folder, then contact customer care with your order number and we'll resend it.

Need it faster?

Express options are offered at checkout where available. For urgent orders in Kuwait, you can also collect from our showroom booth — find our location.
