Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here is how we handle the information you share with Hafeez.

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Hafeez International ("Hafeez", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you shop with us online or visit our booth in Kuwait.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact and delivery details (name, address, email, phone) you provide at checkout.
  • Order history and preferences.
  • Payment confirmation data — card numbers are processed by our payment providers (KNET, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay) and are never stored by Hafeez.
  • Device and usage data collected automatically to keep the site secure and improve it.

3. How we use it

We use your information to process and deliver orders, provide support, prevent fraud, and — only where you have opted in — send you news about new arrivals and offers.

4. Sharing

We share data only with the partners needed to run the store: payment processors, shipping and logistics providers, and the Shopify platform that powers our checkout. We never sell your personal data.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you can unsubscribe from marketing at any time using the link in our emails or by contacting us.

6. Contact

For any privacy request, email hello@hafeez.shop or reach us via the contact details on our About page.
