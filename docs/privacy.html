How Hafeez International collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This policy explains what we collect when you shop with Hafeez International, why we collect it, and the choices you have. By using our store you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Order & account details — name, email, phone, billing and shipping address.
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment providers (KNET, card networks, Apple Pay). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data — pages viewed, device and browser information, collected via cookies to improve the store.

How we use it

  • To process, fulfil and deliver your orders.
  • To provide customer support and handle returns.
  • To send order updates and, with your consent, marketing about new arrivals and drops.
  • To detect fraud and keep the store secure.

Sharing

We share data only with the partners needed to run the store — payment processors, shipping carriers and our e-commerce platform — and only as far as needed to serve you. We never sell your personal information.

Cookies

We use cookies to keep your bag, remember preferences and measure performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some may affect how the store works.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and unsubscribe from marketing at any time. To make a request, email support@hafeez.shop.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@hafeez.shop or via our contact page.
