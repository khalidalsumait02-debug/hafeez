Help centre

Questions?
Answered.

The things customers ask most — shipping, returns, payment, authenticity and sizing.

Where do you ship, and how much does it cost?

We ship across all six GCC countries from Kuwait. Delivery is free on orders over 25 KWD; below that, rates start at 1.500 KWD within Kuwait. See full rates and timings on our Shipping & Delivery page.

How long will my order take?

Orders within Kuwait usually arrive in 1–2 business days. Elsewhere in the GCC it's typically 3–5 business days. You'll get a tracking link by email as soon as your order ships.

How do I track my order?

We email a tracking link when your order ships — use it to follow your parcel. Can't find it? Check spam, then contact us with your order number.

What's your return policy?

You have 14 days from delivery to return unworn items in their original condition. Refunds reach your original payment method within 5–7 business days. Full details on our Returns & Refunds page.

Which payment methods do you accept?

KNET, Visa, Mastercard and Apple Pay, all in Kuwaiti Dinar. Payments are processed securely — we never store your full card details.

Is your Harley-Davidson® merchandise genuine?

Yes. Hafeez is Kuwait's official Harley-Davidson® merchandise reseller, a subsidiary of Harley-Davidson Kuwait. Every item we sell is authentic and officially licensed.

How do I find the right size?

Check our size guides for apparel and headwear. Still unsure? Email us and we'll help you pick.

Can I shop in person?

Absolutely — visit our showroom booth inside Harley-Davidson Kuwait to see the full range. Find our booth.
